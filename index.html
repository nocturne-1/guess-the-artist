Guess the Artist

How to Play

Click load artwork to load up a random artwork.

Type in your guess in the input area. You have five guesses to get it right!

For the artist name, you can type in either first and last name or just last name.

However, spelling does matter, so be sure to you spell their name correctly.

If you guess correctly, you win! Good luck and have fun!
